Who we are?

  • Hello, Namaste everybody

  • My name is Bharat Gihara: I am 43 years old and I am Indian living in New Delhi India.
  • Since over 20 years I am working in the sector of tourism and in my heart, it was always a dream to open my own tourist company. Before my start into the travel business I was a schoolteacher where I taught kindergarten children and I was a tutor for children in the 8th grade of Indian music.
  • In late 1996, I took my very first step, as I got a call from my uncle, who would turn out to be my first boss in the travel industry. He had a small travel agency in Delhi, India and after a brief conversation, my passion towards that industry was set. Everything fell into place and I joined his team in January 1997. It’s amazing to sit back and look at over 20 years in the travel industry, where I’ve held many positions (each more challenging than the previous) and I still get up in the morning and want to go to work!
  • In 2009 I got married and moved to Switzerland, where I was working as a nurse for several years. Since 2017 I am back in New Delhi and setting back on the on the travel business. So, during my stay in Switzerland, I learnt a lot about the standard and the western mindset. The quality and of course I gained new languages.
